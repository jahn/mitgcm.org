Getting Started

To run MITgcm you need to
  • have a suitable computer and compiler,
  • download the code,
  • try to compile and run an example by following the steps on the left.

    • These links will take you to appropriate parts of the documentation.
To help you configure your own experimental set-up, we provide many different example configurations which can be adapted to your particular scenario. The examples include, among others:
  • an idealized sector ocean in spherical coordinates
  • 4°x4° global ocean, with realistic geography and forcing
  • a high resolution simulation of internal waves incident on a slope
  • open ocean deep convection
  • flow over a topographic obstacle
There are also some atmospheric configurations as well as more simplified setups for testing certain aspects of the model such as parameterizations and diagnostics.
