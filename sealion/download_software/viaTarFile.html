Where to get a tar-file?

Before you procede, let us suggest that if you can use CVS then that is the preferred method of download.

We appreciate that not everyone has CVS so we supply gzip'd tar files as in the old days. Note, that even these
files contain CVS information under the sub-directories "CVS". Please don't delete this information because
even if you don't use CVS it can still help us help you.

You'll find tar in our downloads directory.

Be ware that the entire distribution is big. We will be supplying subsets of the distribution to make downloading
easier.
