How to contact us!

The most productive way to sort out a problem is to first check the various sources of in formation.

OK, we give in. You can contact us in several ways:

If you think you have found a bug or major unexpected feature in the code please report it to bugs@mitgcm.org. This will enter your email into a bug-tracking system which means you have us over a barrel if we do nothing. The system will assign you a report number and you can track our progress.

The developers receive email at support@mitgcm.org. The email is also forwarded to the newsgroup for archiving. This is your best chance of getting in contact with us all simultaneously, but the response my be un-coordinated.

Finally, you can contact us directly (ie. privately - our emails are on the users list) but this prohibits others from learning from the process.
